Recipe For Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwhich

Ingredients

2 bread

jelly/Jam


Peanut Butter


Making

1.You are going to take you first slice of bread.

2. On the
first
slice of bread you are going to take some jam/jelly and put it on the bread.

3. Next you are going to take a knife and spread the jam/jelly on the bread.


4. Then you are going to get some peanut butter and spread it on the the other bread slice.


5. You are going to put both the both the slice together. Now you have a peanut butter jelly sandwhcih. Enjoy!
